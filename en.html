EHDV Project

DIVA subunit vaccine against Epizootic Hemorrhagic Disease

Project overview

Development of a DIVA subunit vaccine against Epizootic Hemorrhagic Disease Virus (EHDV-8) using CrisBioÂ® technology.

Main objective

To develop a safe and effective vaccine reducing mortality and morbidity in cattle.

Specific objectives

  • Protection validation in cattle.
  • Industrial antigen production.
  • DIVA analytical tools.
  • Structural and formulation studies.
  • GMP regulatory batches.
  • Clinical trials and EMA dossier.

Funding

Funded by CDTI and AEI, supported by the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.
